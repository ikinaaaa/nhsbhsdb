How To Buy

Set Up Your Wallet

Setup your wallet using metamask or trustwallet to get started. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get Some BNB

Have BSC in your wallet to switch to SHYBA. If you don’t have any BSC, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Buy & Swap For SHYBA

Connect to pancakeswap. Connect your wallet. Paste the SHYBA token address into Pancakeswap or poocoin , select Shyba, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
